ASMSA Concurrent Credit to UAPTC

A spreadsheet displaying the requirements of UAPTC's Associate of Science in Technology and Engineering and what requirements can be fulfilled at ASMSA. Green means that the credit can be attained at ASMSA; red means that it cannot; black represents categories that cannot be attained. Any other colors represent the same course that appears in more than one category in the curriculum.

ASMSA Concurrent Credit Courses

A spreadsheet of all of ASMSA's concurrent credit courses in partnership with UAFS. Twelve of seventy-nine concurrent courses share the same UAFS college course with at least one other ASMSA course. This is represented by the same background color (excluding the cells in indigo).
